McKee Park is the easy, low-effort outdoor spot when you don't want to drive out to a real trail. The walking track loop is nice for a quick run or just clearing your head between classes, and it's mostly flat so it's chill. There are tennis courts that are usually open if you go off-peak, bring your own gear though. Tons of picnic tables and pavilions, people grab them for org cookouts and birthday stuff so on weekends the good shaded ones go early. The playground area means it's family-heavy in the afternoons, just FYI if you wanted a quiet vibe. There's a pond and some open green space that's great for throwing a frisbee or just laying out a blanket. It's well kept and feels safe. Not a wilderness experience by any means, but for a 20-minute reset it does the job perfectly.
