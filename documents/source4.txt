929 is my go-to study cave. The basement seating has this cozy low-light vibe that somehow makes a four-hour grind session feel less miserable. Wifi is solid and there are enough outlets if you grab a spot early. During finals week though? Forget it. Every table is claimed by 9am and people will absolutely guard a table while their stuff sits there for hours, kind of annoying honestly. The cold brew is strong and the lavender latte is the move if you like that floral thing. Baristas are chill and don't rush you out even if you camp. It does get a low buzz of conversation so if you need total silence bring headphones. Prices are reasonable for a coffee shop. Best part is you'll always run into someone you know, which is either great or terrible depending on whether you actually came to study.
