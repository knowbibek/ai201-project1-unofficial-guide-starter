Okay Dave's Dark Horse is a State institution, do not let anyone tell you otherwise. It's a divey, sticky-floor kind of place but that's the whole charm. The pizza is honestly underrated — get the white pizza if you've been sleeping on it, and the calzones are huge. Go on a weeknight if you actually want to hear yourself talk, weekends it's packed with upperclassmen and it gets loud fast. The live music starts late, like 10ish, and it's usually local bands or someone's roommate's band, hit or miss but fun. Pro tip: cash speeds things up at the bar when it's slammed. Bathrooms are rough, you've been warned lol. Parking around there is a nightmare on game weekends so just walk if you're close.
