> top_comment (41 pts): Noxubee National Wildlife Refuge, no contest. The cypress boardwalks are unreal early in the morning. You can spot gators near the water so keep your distance. Bring bug spray or be eaten alive.

>> reply (14 pts): Came here to say Noxubee. Weekday mornings you'll have the whole place to yourself. Trails get muddy after rain though.

> comment (17 pts): If you just want a quick reset and not a whole expedition, McKee Park has a walking loop in town. Not "hiking" but does the job between classes.

>> reply (8 pts): McKee is flat and easy, good for runs. Noxubee is the real nature day.

> comment (5 pts): bird nerds — Noxubee in the cooler months is stacked with waterfowl. so peaceful.
