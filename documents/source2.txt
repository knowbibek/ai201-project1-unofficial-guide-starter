Rick's is THE spot if you want an actual concert energy night out in Starkville. It's way bigger inside than it looks from the parking lot. Weekends it gets absolutely packed so get there before the headliner or you're stuck way in the back not seeing anything. They book a mix of country acts, cover bands, and the occasional bigger touring name, so check who's playing before you commit, the vibe changes a LOT depending on the act. Tickets sell out for the good shows, buy ahead. Drinks are pricey for what they are but that's every venue. It can get sweaty and loud, wear something you don't mind sweating in. Lines at the door after 10 are brutal. Honestly one of the only real live-music nights you can have here without driving to a bigger city.
