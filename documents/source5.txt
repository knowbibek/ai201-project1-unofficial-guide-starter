Two Brothers in the Cotton District is a must, the smoked wings are genuinely the best in town and I will fight people on this. Get them dry rubbed, don't drown them in sauce, let the smoke do the work. The balcony seating is the play on a nice day, you get to people-watch the whole Cotton District strip while you eat. It fills up fast on weekends and after games so expect a wait, no real reservations. The brisket sells out sometimes so go earlier in the day if that's what you're there for. It's a little pricier than typical student food but the portions justify it and it's perfect when your parents are visiting and paying lol. Service can lag when they're slammed, just roll with it. Honestly the whole Cotton District vibe plus that balcony makes it feel less like a college town for a minute.
